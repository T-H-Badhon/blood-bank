Islamic University Blood bank

About

A blood bank is a facility that collects, tests, processes, and stores blood and its components for later use in medical treatments. The primary purpose of a blood bank is to ensure a safe and adequate supply of blood for transfusions and other medical procedures that require blood products.

Blood Doner

A blood donor is an individual who voluntarily donates their blood for the purpose of transfusion to those in need. Blood donation is a selfless act that can help save lives and improve the health of patients undergoing medical treatments or facing emergencies.

Recipient

In the context of blood donation, the duties or responsibilities of a blood recipient are not directly assigned by the blood donor. However, as a blood recipient, there are certain general expectations and responsibilities that can be considered.It's worth noting that these responsibilities are generally considered ethical or voluntary in nature. The primary focus for a blood recipient is on their own health and well-being, following medical guidance, and expressing gratitude for the life-saving gift of donated blood.
